If your blood sugar is above the threshold set by your doctor,
you will be prompted to contact your doctor immediately. Your
doctor's name and phone number will be displayed on the screen.

This prompt does not disable the program. You may proceed as
normal inside the program, whether or not you contact your doctor.

You will be asked about the presence of ketones in your urine.
If you are not sure how to identify ketones, ask your doctor
before proceeding with an answer.
